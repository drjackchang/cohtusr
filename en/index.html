Core Concept

Led by the College of Health Technology at NTUNHS, this project integrates interdisciplinary professional faculty from Nursing, Long-Term Care, Speech-Language Pathology, and Health Care Management.

Leveraging our campus's geographic advantage in the "Group H Uninterruptible Power Zone," we strive to bridge the gap between campus and community needs, extending social resilience through the 4Rs.
