Comprehensive Resilience Empowerment

Building a solid campus safety net from basic first aid to disaster medicine

Basic Response & Shelter Management

Organized practical workshops on "Emergency Response and Basic Bleeding Control" and "Shelter Management" for faculty and students, teaching the core operations of early-stage disaster shelters.

Seed Instructor Training

To ensure sustainability, we held "CPR+AED Instructor Training" and "STB Instructor Training", successfully nurturing 26 community first aid promotion seed instructors.

Disaster Medicine & Nursing

Partnered with NGOs like Taiwan DMAT to host disaster medicine seminars and simulated drills, bringing valuable frontline disaster experiences into education.
